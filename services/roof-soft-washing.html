Roof Soft Washing

Gentle cleaning that removes black streaks, moss, and lichen without damaging shingles.

Schedule this service

Overview

Roof stains are caused by algae growth. Our soft wash system kills the algae at the root and gently rinses it away. This extends the life of your roof and improves energy efficiency.

What's included

  • Safe for all roof types (asphalt, tile, metal)
  • Eliminates black streaks and moss
  • Can increase roof lifespan by years

How it works

1

Roof Inspection

We check for damage and determine the best cleaning method.

2

Soft Wash Application

We apply a biodegradable algaecide that kills growth without high pressure.

3

Gentle Rinse

We rinse the roof with low-pressure water, leaving it clean and streak-free.
