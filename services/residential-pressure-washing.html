Common questions

Will pressure washing damage my siding?

Not when done correctly. We use low-pressure soft wash for siding to avoid damage while still removing dirt and stains.

How often should I have my home pressure washed?

We recommend every 1-2 years to maintain appearance and prevent buildup of harmful organic growth.
